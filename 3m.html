"We already track our digital shelf performance internally with Power BI" ▼

Response: That's exactly the kind of setup we complement rather than replace. Power BI is excellent for visualising and analysing data your team already has. The question is where that data comes from. If your team is manually checking retailer websites or relying on retailer-provided reports, there are gaps - particularly across 20+ retailers in six different channel types. eStore provides the structured, automated data feed that makes your Power BI dashboards more comprehensive and more current. Our API means the data flows directly into your existing analytics environment.

Evidence: We work with several brands that run sophisticated internal analytics - eStore provides the retailer-side data layer they can't generate internally.

Reframe: "We're not replacing your analytics - we're giving them better inputs."

"We already use Salsify/Syndigo for our digital shelf" ▼

Response: Salsify and Syndigo are excellent at what they do - getting product content right at the source and syndicating it to retailers. What they don't provide is visibility into what actually appears on the retailer's website after that content has been processed. Retailers often transform, truncate, or misrender product information. eStore shows you the retailer-side reality: is the content displaying correctly? Are the specifications accurate? How do your listings compare to competitors on the same shelf? We're the retailer-side mirror to your Salsify investment.

Evidence: The confirmed Salsify + Syndigo stack handles PIM and syndication. Neither provides retailer-side search rankings, availability monitoring, competitive benchmarking, or content compliance checking on live retailer pages.

Reframe: "Think of us as completing the loop - Salsify manages what you send, eStore monitors what arrives."

"How many of our retailers can you actually monitor right now?" ▼

Response: Our strongest coverage is across UK and European retailers - which maps well to 3M's Consumer Business Group distribution. We can likely monitor your products across Tesco, Asda, B&Q, Screwfix, Argos, Amazon UK, Amazon DE, Amazon FR, and several other European platforms within weeks. For US retailers like Home Depot, Lowe's, and Target, we'd need to have an honest conversation about timelines - some may require onboarding. We'd recommend starting with your UK and European retailers where we can deliver immediate value, and phasing in additional retailers over time.

Evidence: Many of 3M's core UK/EU retailers are likely already in eStore's network. Estimated time-to-value: 2-4 weeks for UK/EU core monitoring.
