Objection Handling Principles

  • Never dismiss the objection - Acknowledge it genuinely before reframing
  • Be honest about limitations - Especially LATAM coverage and geographic positioning
  • Use specific evidence - Reference their actual retailers, their actual competitive landscape
  • Offer flexibility - Pilots, phased approaches, Mexico-first starts
  • Ask questions - Understand the root concern before responding
  • Don't over-explain - These are sophisticated buyers; respect that
  • Acknowledge previous engagement honestly - If they bring up 2022, don't dodge it
  • Position the distributor model as the key pain point - This is where eStore's value is most tangible for Clarins LATAM
