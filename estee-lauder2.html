Objection Handling Principles

  • Never dismiss the objection - Acknowledge it genuinely before responding
  • Ask questions to understand - The objection often isn't the real concern
  • Use evidence, not claims - Reference specific capabilities, not marketing language
  • Be honest about fit - If there's a genuine gap, acknowledge it
  • Offer flexibility - Pilots, phased approaches, and low-commitment options
  • Don't disparage competitors - Position differentiation without negativity
  • Know when to step back - If timing isn't right, respect that and revisit later

Remember: For a MODERATE FIT account like ELC, the goal isn't to overcome every objection. It's to have an honest conversation about whether there's alignment. Sometimes the answer is "not right now" - and that's a valid outcome.
